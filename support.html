How can you help?

There is no better way to help than saving, recycling and reusing what you already have. Protecting endangered species and cleaning lakes and seas has a positive effect on the environment. At home you can help the planet by recycling waste and growing plants or vegetables. We are the first generation to know we’re destroying the world, and we could be the last that can do anything about it. Speaking up is one of the most powerful things you can do especially if it’s to the right people. Don’t just save what you have, tell other to save as well.

Planting more trees is a great way to help mother nature, but you can’t just go planting these trees all by yourself. Donate on teamtrees.org, Every dollar you donate, a tree will be planted. People like Elon Musk, Jack Dorsey, and many other have helped plant more than 20 million trees, Join this Initiative.
